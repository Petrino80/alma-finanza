Who We Are

Accelerating the Energy Transition

Energy Transition Services (ETS) is a company specialized in the installation, maintenance and upgrade of next-generation wind turbines. Our mission is to make the energy transition safer, faster, and more cost-efficient for developers, operators and OEMs across the globe.

We measure our performance against our core values: LEAN methodologies to continuously improve and the adoption of the latest technologies to innovate across every project we undertake.

With major operating branches in Germany, Italy, Romania, Kosovo and Poland, we deliver high-quality services throughout Europe, the Middle East and North Africa.

What We Do

Our Services

End-to-end wind turbine solutions — from pre-installation through major component upgrades and staffing, delivered with precision across Europe and beyond.

Major Components Exchange

We ensure optimal turbine performance through the replacement of key components such as blades, bearings, generators, gearboxes, hubs, main bearings and yaw systems — minimizing downtime and maximizing energy output.

Pre-Installation & Preventive Maintenance

In the pre-installation phase we provide complete support for concrete and steel towers, along with comprehensive preventive programs including cleaning, lubrication, torquing operations, and UT/DT checks to keep turbines running at peak efficiency.

Staffing

We offer flexible staffing solutions across Europe, supplying qualified technical professionals across the full spectrum of wind turbine installation and major components upgrade activities — right skills, right time, right place.

Track Record

Proven at Scale

With over 750,000 work hours of installation experience, our vision is to accelerate the energy transition, making wind energy more accessible and competitive for every stakeholder in the value chain.

In the last five years, our teams participated in the installation of more than 550 GE units — of which over 300 were large Cypress units — and executed around 1,000 major operations in the same period.

How We Work

Our Core Values

Everything we do is built on three foundational pillars that guide our teams, processes and decisions every day.

01

Safety First

People are at the core of our organisation. We are committed to continuously improving health and safety standards on every site. Our leaders care about their teams, and teams care for each other. We always support the STOP work authority when safety is at risk.

02

Quality & Innovation

With a strong focus on continuous innovation, we use LEAN methodologies, standard processes and highly skilled teams to make every project safer, more efficient and more sustainable. Technology is our enabler, not just our tool.

03

Cost Productivity

Our execution strategy is grounded in driving down costs through process standardisation, rigorous performance measurement, and a constant presence at Gemba — where the value is actually created.
